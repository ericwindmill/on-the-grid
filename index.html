Grid is the newest and most exciting layout tool in CSS3. Use it to play elements virtually anywhere on a webpage.
  • fr - a new unit of measurement that stands for fraction, an fr will take up a fraction of the remaining space of its parent. For example: "400px 2fr 1fr"
  • try minmax(min, max) for column and row sizes!
  • when establishing grid-template-columns or grid-template-rows, try auto-fill
